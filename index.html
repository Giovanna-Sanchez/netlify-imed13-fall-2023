Bread that has the warmth cooked into it

At our bakery, bread is as fresh as can be and filled with the warmth you'd expect to bite into! Our bread is careful made by our wwonderful bakers, filled with a variety of ingrediants that will get you drooling after one bite. Each bread is made to live up to it's name, each varying in different tastes from cultures all around the world.

Here, bread is more than just bread, as baking is more than just baking. We each put our hearts and passions into each loaf we make hoping to share our love with every bite.

Our baking process is carefully carried out, ensuring that each type of bread is carefully cooked for a certain amount of time along with a temperature that is specialized for the variety of bread.

Help us bring our community together

One thing we care about just as much as the care we put into our food, is our customers. We have our bakery open to all sorts of groups of people, welcoming everyone in whether it's for a quick snack or a chat and drink.

We are a shop that operates for majority of the day, open for our 9-5 customers when they need their coffee, open during lunch, and open late at night to provide our nightowl college students a quiet and refreshing environment to work.

We also offer full and part time jobs for those students and anybody else. Please ask in person for a resume if you're interested.
